Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

FinTrack AI ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information — your name and email address when you create an account
  • Financial data — income, expense, and transaction records you manually enter into the app
  • Usage preferences — currency selection and app settings

We do not collect bank account details, card numbers, or connect to any financial institutions. All financial data is entered manually by you.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and operate the FinTrack AI service
  • To sync your data securely across your devices
  • To calculate financial summaries and tax estimates within the app
  • To send you optional local notifications (reminders to log expenses)

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore), which is encrypted in transit and at rest. Access to your data is restricted solely to your authenticated account — no other user or third party can access your financial records.

Firebase services are provided by Google LLC and are subject to Google's Privacy Policy at policies.google.com/privacy.

4. Local Notifications

FinTrack AI may send you local push notifications to remind you to log your daily finances. These notifications are entirely optional and can be disabled at any time through your device settings. No notification data is shared with external servers.

5. Data Export

You can export your transaction data as a CSV file at any time via the Settings screen. This exported data is shared directly from your device using your chosen share method — we do not receive or store a copy of exported files.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at the email address below. Upon request, all data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

7. Children's Privacy

FinTrack AI is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
